Lewis Interiors

Honest, reliable craftsmen

Adam Lewis, Director, is a master builder. With over 30 years' experience, Adam founded Lewis Interiors in 2003, and has an unrivalled reputation in extentions, loft conversions and total refurbishments. 

Lewis Interiors works in partnership with architects, designers, engineers and its clients alike, who value our experience and commitment to producing excellent results.  This has led to repeat business for private clients, who value our reliability, discretion, competitive pricing and total commitment to achieving quality in every aspect of what we do. 

Adam and his colleagues undertook a complete restoration of our three bedroom Victorian house. It comprised a complete interior redesign involving major structural alterations, such as the knocking through of rooms and the removal of chimney breasts and stacks. A loft conversion was also undertaken, and the property re-glazed, re-roofed, re-wired and re-plumbed throughout. Two new bathrooms with underfloor heating were installed, as well as solid oak flooring and an entirely redesigned kitchen. Adam managed the process from start to end, overseeing the development / refinement of structural drawings and liaising with the council for all the relevant approvals. Adam was attentive to detail, highly reliable and clearly has a very keen eye for design. He kept us informed of progress and potential overruns throughout, and worked with us to mitigate and work around these, especially when unforeseen historical structural movement came to light. Overall Adam is a consummate professional and very easy to do business with.
